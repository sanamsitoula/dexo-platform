## 1. Pre-Launch Checklist (fix before going live)

- [ ] **Landing page placeholders** — stats counters currently show "0" and all pricing tiers show "$0/mo". Add real numbers or hide these sections during beta.
- [ ] **Repo hygiene** — `CREDENTIALS.md` is linked from the public README and the license reads "Proprietary and Confidential" on a public repo. Ensure no test credentials match production, and pick a lane: open-source license (attracts contributors) or private repo + public architecture write-up (protects IP).
- [ ] **Public demo tenants** — set up one clickable demo per priority industry (e.g. `demo-fitness.onedexo.com`, `demo-restaurant.onedexo.com`) so prospects can explore without signing up.
- [ ] **Link in bio** — Instagram/X profiles point to onedexo.com (or a simple link page with demo + signup + GitHub).
- [ ] **Demo video** — one 60–90 sec screen recording of tenant provisioning. This single asset gets reused on every platform.

## 2. Campaign Overview

### Goal
50–100 beta tenants across 3–4 priority industries in 60 days, plus 5–10 collaborator / reseller / agency conversations.

### Positioning
> "Launch a fully-branded app and website for your gym, restaurant, salon, or school in minutes — not months. Free during beta. Your feedback shapes the product."

### Priority Segments
| Segment | Why | Channel focus |
|---|---|---|
| **Gym / fitness owners** | Live demo tenant exists (vrfitness); richest vertical (app, payments, QR check-in, AI plans) | Instagram, WhatsApp, Facebook groups |
| **Restaurants & cafés** | Live demo tenant exists (spicegarden); high pain with paper workflows | Instagram, WhatsApp |
| **Salons & schools** | Month 2 expansion; clear booking/fee-management pain | Instagram, WhatsApp |
| **Agencies & freelancers** | White-label resellers — one agency = 10+ tenants | LinkedIn, X |
| **Developers / collaborators** | Contributors, partners on verticals | X, LinkedIn, GitHub |

### Beta Offer
- Free access during beta
- Founding-member discount when pricing launches
- Direct line to the founder for feature requests
- Feedback framed as the "price" of the beta

### Metrics to Track
- Signups per industry
- Activation rate (tenant provisions AND adds real data)
- Feedback submissions
- Agency / collaboration calls booked
- DM keyword responses (GYM / RESTAURANT / SALON / SCHOOL)

## 9. Voice & Style Notes

- **LinkedIn:** founder voice, professional but personal; lead with outcomes, mention tech lightly.
- **WhatsApp:** warm, short, personal; always end with a low-friction offer ("10-minute demo with your logo").
- **Instagram:** visual, emoji-friendly, local; DM keywords as CTA; Nepali voiceovers boost engagement.
- **X:** technical, honest, specific; the niche details (IRD billing, NFRS accounting) are the credibility.
- **Everywhere:** "beta" is a feature, not an apology — feedback is the price, founders' discount is the reward.
