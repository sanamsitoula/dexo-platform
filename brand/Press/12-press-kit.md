# DEXO — Press Kit

## Company overview
DEXO is an open-source, white-label, multi-tenant SaaS platform. It provides the complete "platform layer" — authentication, organizations, role-based access control, subscription billing, custom domains, theming, storage, notifications, and webhooks — behind an API-first core that teams self-host and rebrand as their own. DEXO is MIT licensed and developed in public.
