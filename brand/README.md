# DEXO — Brand & Launch System

The complete brand, design, marketing, and launch package for **DEXO**, the open-source white-label multi-tenant SaaS platform.

**Unified identity at a glance**

- **One-liner:** The open-source white-label platform for launching multi-tenant SaaS.
- **Tagline:** *Ship your SaaS, not your scaffolding.*
- **Primary color:** Dexo Indigo `#4F46E5` · **Accent:** Volt Lime `#A3E635` · **Canvas:** Ink `#09090B`
- **Type:** Space Grotesk (display) · Inter (UI/body) · JetBrains Mono (code)
- **Logo:** "Tenant Cube" — an isometric cube of three stacked planes forming a negative-space **D**, symbolizing isolated tenants on one core.
