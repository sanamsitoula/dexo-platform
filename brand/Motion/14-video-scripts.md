# DEXO — Video Production

## 30-second promo
| Time | Visual | VO / Text |
|---|---|---|
| 0–4s | Rapid montage: devs building the same login/billing screens, clock spinning | "Every SaaS team builds the same thing first. Tenants. Roles. Billing. Months of it." |
| 4–8s | Hard cut to Ink. Stack-assemble logo reveal | "So we built it once. And open-sourced it." |
| 8–18s | Screen capture: `npx create-dexo-app` → dashboard boots → theme engine flips brand live → invoice generated | "DEXO. Multi-tenancy, white-labeling, auth, RBAC, billing — running in minutes, on your infrastructure." |
| 18–25s | Feature icons snap in over isogrid | "API-first. Docker-native. MIT licensed." |
| 25–30s | Mark + tagline + URL | "Ship your SaaS, not your scaffolding. DEXO." |

Music: minimal electronic, 95 BPM, side-chained pad; SFX: soft key clicks, one sub-thump at reveal.

## 60-second promo
Extends the 30s with: 18–30s tenant-isolation diagram animation ("every tenant, walled off"), 30–42s agency use-case ("white-label it — your logo, your domain, your product"), 42–50s community shots (GitHub graph, Discord, contributors).

## Product overview (3–4 min, YouTube)
Chapters: 1) The problem (0:00) 2) Quick start live (0:40) 3) Tenants & orgs (1:20) 4) White-label theme engine (2:00) 5) Billing & RBAC (2:40) 6) API & webhooks (3:10) 7) Deploy & community (3:40). Presenter-led screen capture, picture-in-picture talking head, chapter cards use brand pattern.

## Launch video (Product Hunt, 60–90s)
Founder to camera (15s origin) → product montage (40s, the 60s-promo core) → honest roadmap slide (15s) → CTA (10s). Warm, direct, no stock footage.

## YouTube intro/outro
Intro: 1.5s logo sting. Outro: 8s end-card — mark left, "Subscribe" + two video thumbnails right, URL bottom.

## Demo video (docs, 10 min)
Zero-cut real-time honest walkthrough: install → create tenant → invite team → set roles → connect Stripe → custom domain → rebrand → call the API. Mistakes left in and corrected ("this is the real experience").

## Investor pitch (voiceover skeleton, 3 min)
Problem (undifferentiated SaaS plumbing = $B of wasted eng time) → solution (open-source platform layer) → why now (self-hosting renaissance, AI app boom needs platform substrate) → business model (open core: cloud + enterprise) → traction slide → team → ask.

## Production directions
- **Camera:** talking head at 50mm eq., f/2.8, key light 45°, Ink-toned backdrop with practical indigo accent light.
- **B-roll list:** hands on keyboard macro, terminal scroll, dashboard cursor tours, whiteboard architecture sketch, Discord scroll, git log scroll.
- **Screen capture:** 2560×1440 @ 60fps, cursor smoothing on, brand-demo tenant data only (see `Demo/23-demo-content.md`).
- **Grade:** lift shadows toward indigo, 10% desaturation, match `#09090B` blacks.
- **Captions:** always burned for social (Inter 600, lime keyword highlights).
