# DEXO — Brand Strategy

## Mission
Give every builder the infrastructure of a billion-dollar SaaS company — multi-tenancy, billing, auth, white-labeling — as open source they can own forever.

## Vision
A world where launching a serious SaaS product takes a weekend, not a year, and where the platform layer belongs to the builders, not a vendor.

## Values
1. **Ownership** — your code, your data, your brand. No lock-in, ever.
2. **Craft** — enterprise-grade defaults; production quality is the baseline.
3. **Openness** — built in public, governed transparently, MIT-hearted.
4. **Velocity** — every feature exists to make builders ship faster.
5. **Trust** — tenant isolation, security, and reliability are non-negotiable.

## Positioning
**For** SaaS founders, agencies, and product teams **who** need multi-tenant infrastructure (auth, orgs, RBAC, billing, white-labeling) **DEXO is** an open-source white-label SaaS platform **that** ships the entire boring-but-critical platform layer out of the box, **unlike** closed BaaS vendors or hand-rolled boilerplates, **because** it is self-hostable, API-first, and fully rebrandable.

## USP
The only open-source platform that combines **multi-tenancy + white-labeling + billing + RBAC** as one coherent, self-hostable system — not a boilerplate you fork, a platform you run.

## Brand Story
Every SaaS team rebuilds the same invisible product: tenants, teams, roles, invoices, domains, webhooks. Months of work nobody's customers ever see. DEXO was built on a simple refusal — that the platform layer should be rebuilt even one more time. We took the architecture patterns of the best SaaS companies, made them open source, and wrapped them in an experience developers actually enjoy. You bring the product. DEXO brings the platform.

## Brand Archetype
**The Creator** (primary) with **Sage** undertones — empowering builders with mastery and clear knowledge.

## Voice & Tone
- **Voice:** confident, precise, warm-technical. A senior engineer who's also a good teacher.
- Never hype ("revolutionary", "game-changing"), never cute at the expense of clarity.
- Tone shifts: docs = calm and exact · marketing = bold and concrete · community = friendly and generous · incidents = direct and accountable.

## Descriptions
- **One-line:** The open-source white-label platform for launching multi-tenant SaaS.
- **Elevator pitch (30s):** DEXO is an open-source platform that gives you everything a SaaS needs before your first feature — multi-tenancy, authentication, organizations, RBAC, billing, custom domains, and full white-labeling. Self-host it with Docker or Kubernetes, rebrand every pixel, and build your product on an API-first core instead of spending six months on plumbing.
- **Long description:** DEXO is a production-ready, open-source, white-label multi-tenant SaaS platform. It provides tenant isolation, authentication, organizations and team management, role-based access control, subscription billing, custom domains, a theme engine, notifications, storage, webhooks, analytics, and an admin panel — all behind a documented REST API. Deploy anywhere with Docker or Kubernetes, extend it with plugins, localize it for any market, and put your own brand on every surface. DEXO is the platform layer you were about to build, already built.

## Problem → Solution
- **Problem:** Teams spend 3–9 months rebuilding identical multi-tenant infrastructure before writing a single differentiating feature; closed platforms solve this at the cost of lock-in, per-MAU pricing, and no brand control.
- **Solution:** An open-source, self-hostable platform core with white-labeling as a first-class feature — the speed of a BaaS with the ownership of your own code.

## Writing & Copy Rules
- Sentence case everywhere, including headlines and buttons. Oxford comma. No exclamation marks in product UI.
- Active voice; second person ("you deploy", not "users can deploy"). Numerals for all numbers ≥ 2.
- Product terms capitalized once defined: Tenant, Organization, Workspace, Plan.
- Concrete beats abstract: "deploy in 4 commands" not "deploy effortlessly".

## CTA Style
- Primary CTAs are verbs + object, 2–4 words: **Start building** · **Deploy DEXO** · **Read the docs** · **Star on GitHub**.
- One primary CTA per view. Secondary CTA is always a ghost/link style. Never "Learn more" alone — always "See how billing works"-style specificity.
